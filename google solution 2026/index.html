Trusted workflow

Invisible watermarking + blockchain proof + AI detection

TruthMark creates a tamper-evident content lifecycle so creators and consumers can verify the origin and integrity of images and videos.

How it works

Three layers of digital trust

1

Proactive Protection

Embed an invisible cryptographic watermark into the media's frequency domain, preserving creator identity and date while surviving compression, cropping, and filters.

2

Decentralized Proof

Register a content hash on Polygon to generate a public Certificate of Authenticity and create an immutable chain of custody.

3

Reactive Verification

Use AI-powered analysis to detect missing watermarks, lighting anomalies, and GAN artifacts for real-time authenticity scoring.

Why TruthMark?

Designed to restore confidence in digital media.

Creators gain ownership control, consumers get transparent verification, and platforms can enforce integrity without depending on centralized trust alone.

  • Creator-first protection: Invisible fingerprints that travel with every image and video.
  • Immutable certificates: Blockchain-backed proof of authenticity and provenance.
  • AI-first verification: Deep learning checks for manipulated content and synthetic artifacts.
